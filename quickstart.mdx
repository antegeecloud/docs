Welcome to Edgee, the cutting-edge technology platform designed to paves the way to the next phase of the internet.
Edgee was founded on the realization that several traditional client-side technologies don't work for a significant portion of user, primarily due to legal constraints, technical limitations, and the widespread use of ad blockers.
Our platform is built to address these challenges by moving client-side SDKs to the edge, close to the user, ensuring real-time processing, anonymity, and compliance with global data protection laws.
This guide will walk you through the basics of getting started with Edgee, from setting up your account to implementing our libraries in your applications.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit the [Edgee console registration page](https://console.edgee.cloud/register)
2. Fill in your details, including your name, email, and password.
3. Please, be aware that creating an account means that you agree to our [Terms of Service](https://www.edgee.cloud/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.edgee.cloud/privacy).
4. You will receive an email where you will have to click on a link to confirm your email address.
