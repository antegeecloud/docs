---
title: Personal account
description: Learn what an Personal Account is and how to create and manage it
---

A Personal Account is a user account that is created by an individual for personal use. 
It is a free account that allows you to access the platform and use its features. 

You can create a Personal Account by signing up on the platform. Once you have created an account, 
you can log in to the platform and start creating your first edge project.
