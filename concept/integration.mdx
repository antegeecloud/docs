---
title: Integration
description: Learn about integrations in Edgee.
---

Integrations are third-party services and tools that enhance the functionality of your edge applications.
Edgee supports a variety of integrations, such as analytics, security, and monitoring services.

By leveraging integrations, you can extend the capabilities of your edge applications 
without the need for extensive custom development. Edgee offers a variety of pre-built integrations, 
such as [Google Analytics](/integrations/data-collection/google-analytics), 
[Amplitude](/integrations/data-collection/amplitude), [Google Analytics](/integrations/ab-testing/statsig), 
and [Datadome](/integrations/security/datadome), simplifying the process of 
enhancing your applications with powerful external services.
