<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Edgee?">
    Edgee is an innovative full-stack edge platform that allows developers to deploy and manage services closer to users,
    enhancing performance, security, and scalability by leveraging a global network of edge locations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Edgee improve application performance?">
    By processing data and requests at the edge of the network — closer to the user — Edgee reduces latency, frees up client-side resources,
    speeds up content delivery, and ensures a smoother user experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What types of applications can benefit from Edgee?">
    Any web-based application can benefit from Edgee, especially those requiring real-time data processing,
    high availability, global reach, and security, such as e-commerce sites, streaming services, online gaming, and IoT applications.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get started with Edgee?">
    Getting started with Edgee is easy. Simply sign up on our website, follow the setup guide in our documentation,
    and you can begin deploying your applications on our full-stack edge platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Edgee with my existing CDN?">
    Yes, Edgee can complement your existing CDN by providing advanced edge computing capabilities.
    If your CDN is partnered with Edgee, you can seamlessly integrate our services. Contact our sales team for more information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What components does Edgee offer?">
    Edgee supports a wide range of components with leading technologies in analytics, AB testing, and security,
    including Google Analytics, Amplitude, Piano Analytics, Segment, Statsig, and Datadome. More components are being added regularly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Edgee secure?">
    Absolutely. Security is a top priority at Edgee. We offer robust security features, including DDoS protection, bot management,
    and secure data processing, to ensure your applications and data are protected at the edge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Edgee priced?">
    Edgee offers flexible pricing plans tailored to your specific usage and needs, including pay-as-you-go and subscription options.
    For detailed pricing information, please visit our pricing page or contact our sales team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I contact support?">
    Our support team is here to help you with any questions or issues.
    You can contact us by email: support@edgee.cloud
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I self-host Edgee?">
    Yes, while we recommend using Edgee as a managed service for ease of use and optimal performance,
    you have the option to self-host our platform. This is particularly useful for integrating Edgee within specific environments, like Kubernetes clusters.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
